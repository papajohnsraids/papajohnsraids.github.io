Recruiting

Are you interested in selling raids? What a coincidence because we are interested in recruiting you!


Some baseline requirements we are looking for in our applicants:
  • Advanced knowledge in all of the raid encounters and the classes you play
  • Can play on the NA servers
  • Can play a variety of support classes at a high level
  • Very experienced at doing the various mechanics your support class is required to do
OR
  • Be a big dps pumper. Like 85-95% bench for all the dps classes that you play is ideal

We don't have a fixed schedule because we are flexible for our buyers, so that means there is no commitment either. If there is a sale for the day and you are available you can sign up (or not) and we choose from all of the raiders that have signed up. We also always raid at 8pm EST


Sounds good to you? Go ahead and apply! Send us logs that you are proud of, and we will consider you!
